Orders & Shipping

How long does shipping take?

Shipping times depend on your location and the shipping method selected:

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days
  • Same-Day Delivery: Available in select cities

You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships.

Can I track my order?

Yes! You can track your order in several ways:

  • Visit the My Orders section in your account
  • Use the tracking link sent to your email
  • Enter your order number on our order tracking page

You'll see real-time updates from order confirmation to delivery.

What if my order is delayed?

If your order is delayed beyond the estimated delivery date:

  • Check your order tracking for updates
  • Contact our support team for assistance
  • We'll provide compensation for significant delays

Payments & Refunds

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple secure payment methods:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, RuPay)
  • UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.)
  • Net Banking
  • Digital Wallets (Paytm, Amazon Pay)
  • Cash on Delivery (COD)

All payments are processed securely through Razorpay.

How long do refunds take?

Refund processing times vary by payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-7 business days
  • UPI/Digital Wallets: 1-3 business days
  • Net Banking: 3-5 business days
  • COD Orders: Bank transfer within 7 days

You'll receive email confirmation once the refund is processed.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely! We prioritize your security:

  • SSL encryption for all transactions
  • PCI DSS compliant payment processing
  • We never store your card details
  • Two-factor authentication available

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?

Our return policy is designed to be customer-friendly:

  • 7-day return window from delivery date
  • Items must be in original condition with tags
  • Free returns for defective/wrong items
  • Easy online return request process

Some items like undergarments and perishables cannot be returned for hygiene reasons.

How do I initiate a return?

Returning items is simple:

  • Go to your order history
  • Select the order and click "Return Items"
  • Choose items and reason for return
  • Schedule pickup or drop-off

You'll receive a return tracking number and regular updates.

Can I exchange an item instead of returning it?

Yes! We offer exchanges for:

  • Different sizes of the same product
  • Different colors (subject to availability)
  • Defective items for new ones

Select "Exchange" when initiating your return request.

Account & Profile

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is quick and easy:

  • Click "Sign Up" in the top navigation
  • Enter your email, name, and password
  • Verify your email address
  • Complete your profile information

Having an account lets you track orders, save addresses, and get personalized recommendations.

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

To reset your password:

  • Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
  • Enter your registered email address
  • Check your email for reset instructions
  • Click the link and create a new password
Can I change my email address?

Yes, you can update your email address:

  • Go to your profile settings
  • Click "Edit Profile"
  • Update your email address
  • Verify the new email address

Technical Support

The website is loading slowly. What should I do?

Try these troubleshooting steps:

  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Try a different browser or incognito mode
  • Check your internet connection
  • Disable browser extensions temporarily

If the problem persists, contact our technical support team.

I'm having trouble placing an order. What should I do?

Common solutions for checkout issues:

  • Ensure all required fields are filled correctly
  • Check that your payment method is valid
  • Try a different payment method
  • Clear your cart and try again

Contact support if you continue experiencing issues.

Do you have a mobile app?

Currently, we don't have a dedicated mobile app, but our website is fully optimized for mobile devices:

  • Responsive design works on all screen sizes
  • Fast loading on mobile networks
  • Touch-friendly interface
  • Mobile-optimized checkout process

A mobile app is in development and will be available soon!
