Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?

You can track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "My Orders" section. You'll receive tracking updates via email and SMS as well.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Products must be unused and in their original packaging. Some items may have different return conditions.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. You can check availability during checkout.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team through email, phone, or live chat. We typically respond within 24 hours during business days.

Are my payment details secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard SSL encryption to protect your payment information. We currently accept UPI and Cash on Delivery. We never store sensitive payment details on our servers.
